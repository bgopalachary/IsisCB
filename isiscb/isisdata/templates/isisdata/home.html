IsisCB Explore...

IsisCB Explore... is a research tool for the history of science, whose core dataset comes from bibliographical citations in the Isis Bibliography of the History of Science. The IsisCB currently contains 40-years of citation data from 1974 to 2014. It will soon be expanded to include bibliographic entries going back to 1913. Use the search bar above to start exploring the IsisCB database!
